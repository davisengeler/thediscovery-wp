My strengths

I find it hard to talk about my own skillset, but I understand that it is an important way to judge both my character and give an inside-out perspective of my strengths and focal points. I would rather be underestimated and pleasantly surprise someone than be overestimated and disappoint them. Nevertheless, here are some of my key points:

FUNDAMENTAL EFFICIENCY

Clean looking code is not sufficient. I have focused my efforts on building efficient development structures, inside and out. I see huge benefits in considering the deeper aspects of problems and their solutions.

COLLABORATION IN MIND

I plan my development around collaboration, even if I'm working on a project independently. Whether it's future interoperability, team collaboration, new developers on board, or even a forgetful self, this style is often a huge benefit down the road.
